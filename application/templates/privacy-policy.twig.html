When you visit the application, it may collect any information you have made visible to "Everyone" on Facebook as well as the information Facebook designates as your publicly available information. We may share this information on your wall or with other users without your explicit approval.

The application may ask you to explicitly provide certain information to us, such as your email address from a pre-defined list to send to your friends. We generally use Independent Information for the same purposes as we use Information We Receive From Facebook.
